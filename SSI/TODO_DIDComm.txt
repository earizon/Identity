# DIDComm
* <https://identity.foundation/didcomm-messaging/spec/>

When compared: 
* OIDC4SSI / SIOP (Self-Issued OpenID Connect Provider): An extension 
  of a mature authentication and authorisation protocol called "OpenID 
  Connect" (OIDC).
* DIDComm: A novel protocol specifically designed for SSI and 
  maintained by the Decentralized Identity Foundation (DIF).
* Credential Handler API: A proposed browser-extension that may be 
  used to connect the user's identity wallet to a web-application.

* Extracted from Pushed Auth. Request (PAR) article.

   demands. Some of such specifications make authorization requests 
   bigger.

   request parameter, authorization_details, which has a complex JSON 
   structure ...
   ... OpenID Connect for Identity Assurance 1.0” (IDA) (my article:
   “Identity Assurance — OpenID Connect in the eKYC era”
   <https://darutk.medium.com/identity-assurance-openid-connect-in-the-ekyc-era-3be78384a1d1>)
